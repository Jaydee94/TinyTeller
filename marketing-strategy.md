# TinyTeller — Marketing Strategy

## Objective
Acquire 1,000 engaged users within 60 days of public launch; validate paid conversion for Plus tier.

## Target Audience
- Primary: Parents of 2–4 year olds (decision fatigue; looking for quick, playful routines)
- Secondary: Early childhood educators and caregivers

## Key Insight
- Beta (50 families) showed 87% of button presses were parents (not children) using TinyTeller to simplify decisions.

## Positioning
- Product: A quick, playful decision helper that turns everyday choices into short, parent-approved stories and activities.
- Value prop: Reduce decision friction; turn moments of overwhelm into tiny, delightful interactions.

## Pricing & Business Model
- Free tier: Basic story seeds and limited daily uses
- Plus tier: $4.99/month — full story library, custom voice & content packs, analytics and family profiles

## Channels & Tactics
- Organic: SEO-focused landing pages (parenting keywords), blog posts with parenting resources
- Paid: Facebook/Instagram (parenting groups), TikTok short demos, Search Ads for parenting queries
- Partnerships: Parenting newsletters, daycare networks, pediatrician offices
- Press: Target tech-for-good and parenting outlets for launch announcements
- Beta referrals: incentivize referring families with free Plus trial days

## Launch KPIs
- 1,000 signups in 60 days
- 10% activation rate (users performing at least 3 story plays in first week)
- 3–5% paid conversion within first 90 days

## Initial 60-day Plan (high level)
1. Pre-launch (weeks 0–2): Landing page, email capture, press list and outreach templates
2. Soft launch (weeks 3–4): Invite beta families, collect qualitative feedback, iterate stories
3. Public Launch (weeks 5–8): Paid ads, influencer demos, press outreach
4. Growth (weeks 9–12): Referral push, partnerships, product improvements

## Metrics & Analytics
- Track: daily active users, session length, story completion rate, retention (D7, D30), referral conversions
- Use simple dashboard (Metabase/Google Analytics + Stripe for payments)

## Creative Assets Needed
- App screenshots and short demo videos (15–30s)
- Social creative templates (square + vertical)
- Press images and copy

## Risks & Mitigations
- Risk: Low paid conversion — Mitigate with stronger onboarding, trial experience, and targeted promotions
- Risk: Misaligned expectations (parents expecting educational outcomes) — Mitigate with clear landing page copy and FAQs
