# TinyTeller ✨
**Instant magic for toddlers and parents**

TinyTeller is a minimalist web application designed to bring instant magic to toddlers and their parents. With a single interaction—the "Magic Button"—the app generates and displays bite-sized, age-appropriate stories tailored for the 2-to-4-year-old demographic.

## 🌟 **Key Features**

- **One-Tap Magic:** No complex menus. One click delivers a fresh, engaging short story instantly.
- **Toddler-Centric Content:** Stories are focused on simple themes like animals, friendship, and daily routines, designed to be read in under two minutes.
- **Distraction-Free UI:** A clean, calming interface with high-contrast elements and large buttons, making it easy for parents to use even in low-light "bedtime" settings.

## 🎯 For Parents

TinyTeller solves the bedtime story dilemma:
- **No preparation needed** - stories appear instantly
- **Age-appropriate content** - safe and suitable for 2-4 year olds
- **Perfect for busy parents** - quality content in seconds, not minutes
- **Bedtime friendly** - high contrast UI for dark rooms, calming colors

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Visit the TinyTeller app
2. Press the Magic Button ✨
3. Read a new story to your toddler
4. Repeat anytime - new stories appear instantly

## 💖 The Magic

Every tap creates a moment of connection. One button, endless stories, countless bedtime memories.
